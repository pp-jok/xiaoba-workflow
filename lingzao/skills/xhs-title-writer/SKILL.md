Default to 3 strongest Lingzao / XHS title options, not 10 loose options.

## Inputs

- topic or draft
- target audience
- note format: graphic note, spoken video, Vlog, product, or local life
- cover copy or reference note if available

## Title Rules

A good title should usually have:

- a concrete keyword anchor
- a clear click reason
- a truthful promise
- audience or scene fit
- cover consistency

Common useful patterns:

- specific identity + result
- time + transformation
- city/track keyword + guide
- pain + method
- curiosity + concrete object
- contrast before/after

## Output

Give 3 titles:

- title
- keyword anchor
- why users may click
- what the content must deliver so the title is not empty

Do not over-polish into generic motivational wording.
